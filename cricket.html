Why is cricket popular in india

  • Cricket is popular in India because of the fact that it was a provinces in the British Empire
  • Britain invented cricket, the British colonials naturally played it and so it spread into the culture of the respective countries for all citizens.

Gully Cricket

  • It is an informal ad hoc variant of the game of cricket played by people of all genders and all age
  • It is played in gardens, back yards, on the street, in parks, carparks, beaches and any area not specifically intended for the purpose.

IPL

  • The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested during March or April and May of every year
  • It has eight teams representing eight different cities in India.
  • The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India in 2008.
  • The IPL is the most attended cricket league in the world and rank sixth among all sports leagues.
