Universal studio







Home to some of the world’s most advanced and amazing attractions and rides, Universal Orlando Resort allows guests to experience favorite films and television shows like never before. Join the fight between Autobots and Decepticons in TRANSFORMERS: The Ride-3D, become a loveable minion on Despicable Me Minion Mayhem, hang out with Spidey on The Amazing Adventures of Spider-Man or come face-to-face with a tyrannosaurus rex on the Jurassic Park River Adventure.Walking around the parks will really work up an appetite and Universal Orlando Resort has great eats for everyone, including treats inspired by some of your favorite television programs and films.Once the sun goes down, the excitement picks up at Universal CityWalk, the dining, shopping and entertainment district at the Universal Orlando Resort.You don’t have to wait for your letter to Hogwarts. Universal Orlando Resort is the place for wizards and muggles alike with The Wizarding World of Harry Potter.
